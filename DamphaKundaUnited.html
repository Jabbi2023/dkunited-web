Dampha Kunda United UK Overview

Dampha Kunda United in UK was officially established in January 2020 by the founding members as follow: 1. Jammeh Drammeh 2. Edrisa Ceesay 3. Musa Dibassy 4. Alhige Turo Ceesay 5. Musa Susso 6. Mahamadou Jabbi 7. Alhige Solo Ceesay 8. Kalilu Ceesay 9. Alhige Jabbi.

The group comprises native of Dampha Kunda Village, Basse, Upper River Region, the Gambia, West Africa who are currently based in the United Kingdom coming together as one, united under one banner and formed a group that will inspire generations.

Group membership is not only limited to the native of Dampha Kunda Village currently based in UK, but to everyone who has profound interest in the group agenda is welcome to join.
