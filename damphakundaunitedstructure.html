Dampha Kunda United Organisational Structure

In order for an organisation to achieve its overall corporate goals and objectives, there should be in place an effective board committee who set the directions, monitor and manage the affairs of an organisation. The directors or board committee are elected by the shareholders of an organisation. The board members conduct meetings periodically to discuss issues affecting the organisation and make key decisions. Dampha Kunda United UK Board structure will include the following:
1. Chairman: Edrisa Ceesay
2. Vice chairman: Jammeh Drammeh
3. Secretary: Mahamadou Jabbi
4. Treasury: Musa Dibassy
5. Public Relation Officer:Alhige Solo Ceesay
Any member interested in the above position can come forward to express their interest and the group will conduct a democratic election to choose who held what position.
