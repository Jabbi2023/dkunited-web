Introduction

INTRODUCTION Welcome to Dampha Kunda United in United Kingdom group, it involves native of Dampha Kunda village who are currently based in the United Kingdom coming together as one, united under one banner and formed a group that will inspire generations. The group membership is not limited to only the native of Dampha Kunda but anyone who has a profound interest in the group is welcome to join. Dampha Kunda United in UK was officially established in January 2020 by the founding members as follow: 1. Jammeh Drammeh 2. Edrisa Ceesay 3. Musa Dibassy 4. Alhige Turo Ceesay 5. Musa Susso 6. Mahamadou Jabbi 7. Alhige Solo Ceesay 8. Kalilu Ceesay 9. Alhige Ceesay
